Step 04

It searches Google Flights for real prices

The agent connects to Google Flights to get live, up-to-date prices — the same ones you'd see if you searched yourself. It only looks at official airlines like KLM, United, Lufthansa and Iberia. No third-party booking sites.

Think of it like…

Having someone open Google Flights on your behalf every night, search all the combinations, and write down every price they see. Same data, zero effort from you.

🔐

Your data stays yours

The agent only stores anonymised price data for comparison purposes. It never stores your payment details, passport info, or personal data of any kind.

💸

Practically free to run

The agent runs on free or near-free services. The AI analysis costs less than €0.50 a month at this usage. You only pay if you choose to upgrade for more airports.

🛑

It never books for you

The agent finds deals and tells you about them. Every booking decision — and every click — is yours. It cannot and will not purchase anything on your behalf.
